Welcome to Red Labyrinth!

Can you complete all the cryptic puzzles and make it out of the Red Labyrinth?

About

This event is a virtual treasure hunt where one challenge leads to another.
Contestants are required to crack these cryptic challenges using their basic IT knowledge and problem-solving skills. The fastest teams to crack these challenges will be declared winners!

This challenge will definitely put one’s critical thinking and problem-solving skills to a test!

Rules

  • Contestants are allowed to use internet browsers to search for answers and solutions.
  • Contestants are not supposed to share answers with contestants from other teams.
  • A team cannot have more than 3 contestants. (Contestants can participate in teams of 1,2 and 3).
  • Each team will be required to make an account on the "The Red Labyrinth" website to take part in the challenge.
    Team members can then share the login credentials with each other.
  • Each team cannot have more than one account on the challenge website.
