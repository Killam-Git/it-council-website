BGMI Rules

  • Only Mobile Phones/ iPads/ Tablets are allowed, no emulators.
  • There are no restrictions on guns and resources as such.
  • The players are strictly not allowed to use any forms of hacks or any other software/tool which gives them undue advantage. Any team found using any hack will be banned from the tournament immediately.
  • Players must not engage with other players in a verbal manner (curse words, racism, and sexism).
  • All players must install the latest version of the game in order to participate in the tournament. Any update must be installed before the tournament starts, so delays will be at a minimum
  • Any form of collusion/ cheating/ exploitation of bugs by any Team Members is prohibited
  • No substitutes allowed in the middle of the games.
  • The username given should match when once in lobby , otherwise it may result in ban from the tournament.
  • In-case of a server crash, the respective round will be re-started.
  • It is expected that all players will abide by the rules and regulations of the tournament in order to make this event a fun
    experience for all.
