Welcome to Trivia

Think you know it all? Put your IT knowledge to a test!

About

This event will be conducted in the form of a quiz having two rounds.
The first round will consist of a trivia quiz where contestants will have to answer tech-related questions in a Multiple Choice Question format. Those who qualify for the second round will have to take part in a Round Robin Questionnaire session.

The contestants that bag the most number of points in this round shall be declared as the winners!

Rules

  • This event will consist of 2 rounds.
  • Contestants will have to be on a zoom call while attending the first round.
  • Contestants will be provided a form with all the trivia questions and must submit their answers within the given
    time period of the quiz.
  • Contestants are not supposed to use any sort of external help (internet browsers, books, other individuals, etc).
  • Those who have been shortlisted for the second round which is the 'Round Robin Questionnaire Session' must
    join a zoom call and keep their camera switched on throughout the whole round.
